Graphics Projects

Double Exposure Image Project

The double exposure image project involves creating visually captivating images by blending two or more photographs into a single composition. This technique overlays one image on top of another, allowing both to be seen simultaneously, creating a surreal and artistic effect. Typically, one image serves as the background, while the other is overlaid with reduced opacity to blend seamlessly. This project requires a keen eye for aesthetics, an understanding of image composition, and proficiency in photo editing software like Photoshop. The result is often a striking and dreamlike visual that conveys complex themes and emotions.

Stickers Making Project

The stickers making project focuses on designing and creating custom stickers for various purposes, such as personal expression, branding, or promotional materials. This involves conceptualizing unique designs, often utilizing graphic design software like Adobe Illustrator or Photoshop. The project encompasses various stages, including sketching ideas, digitizing designs, and preparing them for printing. Stickers can range from simple text and logos to intricate illustrations. This project enhances creativity, design skills, and understanding of print production processes, culminating in tangible, adhesive art pieces that can be used on a variety of surfaces.
